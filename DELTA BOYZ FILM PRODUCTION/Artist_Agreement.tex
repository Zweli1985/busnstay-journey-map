This Artist Employment and Profit-Sharing Agreement (the ``Agreement'') is entered into on this 26th day of August, 2025, by and between:

\textbf{Delta Boy’s Production (Pty) Ltd} (hereinafter referred to as ``Delta'' or the ``Company''), a private limited company duly incorporated under the laws of the Republic of South Africa, with its principal place of business at Fourways, Gauteng, South Africa, represented herein by Mr. Cleveland Harris, its Managing Director,

and

\textbf{Miss Yolanda Ruiters} (hereinafter referred to as the ``Artist'' or ``Yasirah Bhelz''), an individual residing at Fourways, Gauteng, South Africa, performing under the stage name ``Yasirah Bhelz'' as a music artist and podcast creator.

(Collectively referred to as the ``Parties'' and individually as a ``Party'').

\section{RECITALS}

WHEREAS, Delta is a recording studio specializing in music production, operating in South Africa and internationally;

WHEREAS, the Artist has facilitated the purchase of a property located in Fourways, South Africa (the ``Property''), on behalf of Delta, including assisting with furnishing and procuring studio equipment necessary for music production;

WHEREAS, all costs associated with the purchase, furnishing, and equipment procurement for the Property have been fully settled by Delta;

WHEREAS, Delta seeks to engage the Artist to record, perform, and participate in music and podcast projects produced by or affiliated with Delta;

WHEREAS, the Artist, under her stage name Yasirah Bhelz, is an established performer in music and will contribute to Delta’s productions while pursuing personal creative projects, such as podcasts;

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to formalize the terms of the Artist’s engagement, profit-sharing arrangements, and ancillary benefits, including \textbf{guaranteed complete occupation and usage of the Property, including stay, for the Artist during the term of this Agreement, with the right to remain for the full five (5) year term as long as she fulfills her musical commitments, and only under extreme circumstances may she be required to vacate, with a minimum of ninety (90) days' notice};

WHEREAS, the Parties intend for this Agreement to be governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, with exclusive jurisdiction in South African courts;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises, covenants, and agreements contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

\section{DEFINITIONS}

For the purposes of this Agreement:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Artist}: Miss Yolanda Ruiters, professionally known as Yasirah Bhelz.
    \item \textbf{Music Profits}: Net profits, calculated after deducting all direct costs (e.g., production, distribution, and marketing expenses), derived from music-related activities of Delta involving the Artist, specifically limited to songs released with the Artist as the credited performer.
    \item \textbf{Podcast Profits}: Net profits, calculated after deducting all direct costs (e.g., production and hosting expenses), derived from podcast-related activities produced and presented by the Artist in her personal capacity but facilitated through Delta’s resources.
    \item \textbf{Property}: The real estate property located in Fourways, South Africa, purchased by Delta with the Artist’s facilitation, including all studio equipment therein.
    \item \textbf{Tenure}: The period during which the Artist is engaged by Delta under this Agreement.
    \item \textbf{Confidential Information}: Any non-public information relating to Delta’s business, including but not limited to financial data, client lists, and proprietary processes.
\end{enumerate}

\section{ENGAGEMENT AND ROLE}

\subsection{Engagement as Music Artist and Podcast Creator}
Delta hereby engages the Artist to perform, record, and participate in music and podcast projects for the Company, effective from the date of this Agreement.

\subsection{Duties and Responsibilities}
The Artist shall:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Perform in music recordings and live shows as reasonably requested by Delta, in accordance with industry standards.
    \item Collaborate on music projects produced or managed by Delta, contributing creative input as mutually agreed.
    \item Represent Delta professionally in promotional and public engagements when mutually agreed.
    \item Produce and present podcasts in her personal capacity, with the option to utilize Delta’s studio resources, subject to availability and reimbursement of incremental costs incurred by Delta.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Exclusivity}
During the Tenure, the Artist shall not engage in music production activities that directly compete with Delta’s core business in South Africa without prior written consent from Delta. This restriction does not apply to the Artist’s personal podcast projects or non-competing creative endeavors.

\section{COMPENSATION AND PROFIT-SHARING}

\subsection{Profit-Sharing}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Music Profits}: The Artist shall receive 50\% of Music Profits, applicable only to songs released with the Artist as the credited performer, payable quarterly within 30 days following the end of each fiscal quarter, accompanied by a detailed accounting statement.
    \item \textbf{Podcast Profits}: The Artist shall receive 70\% of Podcast Profits, with Delta retaining 30\%, payable quarterly within 30 days following the end of each fiscal quarter, accompanied by a detailed accounting statement.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Film Participation}
Separate profit-sharing or compensation arrangements for the Artist’s participation in film projects produced by Delta may be negotiated and agreed upon in writing by both Parties, independent of the Music Profits terms outlined above.

\subsection{Expenses}
Delta shall reimburse the Artist for all reasonable, pre-approved business expenses incurred in the performance of her duties, provided the Artist submits receipts and documentation within 30 days of incurring such expenses.

\section{BENEFITS}

\subsection{Guaranteed Occupation and Usage of Property}
During the Tenure, and provided the Artist fulfills her musical commitments under this Agreement, the Artist shall have \textbf{guaranteed complete occupation and usage of the Property, including full residential stay and access to all studio facilities, utilities, maintenance, and common areas}, at no cost to the Artist for an initial period of five (5) years. The Artist may not be removed from the Property during this five (5) year term except under extreme circumstances (such as proven gross misconduct or criminal activity), and only with a minimum of ninety (90) days' written notice. Delta shall be responsible for property taxes, insurance, and major repairs, excluding damages caused by the Artist’s negligence or willful misconduct.

\subsection{Termination of Occupancy}
If, under extreme circumstances as described above, the Agreement is lawfully terminated, the Artist shall vacate the Property only after receiving a minimum of ninety (90) days' written notice, returning it in good condition, subject to normal wear and tear. Delta reserves the right to inspect the Property upon vacation.

\subsection{Other Benefits}
The Artist shall be eligible for benefits in accordance with South African labour law, including but not limited to medical aid, pension contributions, and a minimum of 21 consecutive days of annual leave per year, subject to Delta’s standard policies. The Parties may mutually agree in writing to increase benefits in the future, contingent upon Delta’s business demonstrating sustained positive cash flow and financial stability, with such adjustments to be documented as amendments to this Agreement.

\section{INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY}

\subsection{Ownership}
All intellectual property, including but not limited to recordings, compositions, and performances, created by the Artist in connection with Delta’s projects or using Delta’s resources, shall remain the \textbf{sole and exclusive property of the Artist}. The Artist grants Delta a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, and distribute such works only for the purposes of Delta’s projects as mutually agreed. Delta shall have no ownership claim over any intellectual property created by the Artist.

\subsection{Personal Projects}
Podcasts and all other works produced and presented by the Artist in her personal capacity shall remain her sole intellectual property. Delta shall have no ownership claim over such works, subject to the agreed profit-sharing terms in Section 3.1.

\subsection{Promotional Use of Name and Likeness}
The Artist hereby grants Delta a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use her name, stage name (Yasirah Bhelz), likeness, and photographs in promotional materials, including but not limited to the Delta Boy’s Travelware website, for the purpose of promoting Delta’s projects, services, or products during the Tenure of this Agreement. The Artist retains the right to review and approve the specific use of her name and likeness in promotional materials, such approval not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed.

\section{CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-COMPETE}

\subsection{Confidentiality}
The Artist agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information during and for five (5) years following the termination of this Agreement. The Artist shall not disclose such information to third parties without Delta’s prior written consent, except as required by law.

\subsection{Non-Compete}
For twelve (12) months following termination of this Agreement, the Artist shall not engage in music or film production activities with direct competitors of Delta in South Africa, unless termination is without cause by Delta or by mutual agreement.

\section{TERM AND TERMINATION}

\subsection{Term}
This Agreement shall commence on the date hereof and continue for a period of five (5) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Section, with an option for renewal upon mutual written agreement of the Parties.

\subsection{Termination}
This Agreement may be terminated:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item By mutual written agreement of the Parties.
    \item By Delta only for extreme circumstances (such as proven gross misconduct or criminal activity) with a minimum of ninety (90) days' written notice to the Artist.
    \item By the Artist with 60 days’ written notice.
    \item Automatically upon the death, permanent incapacity, or insolvency of either Party.
\end{enumerate}

Upon termination, all obligations regarding profit-sharing, confidentiality, and non-compete clauses shall survive as specified herein.

\section{GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION}

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa. Any disputes arising under or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of South Africa.

\section{MISCELLANEOUS}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Entire Agreement}: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter herein.
    \item \textbf{Amendments}: Any amendments to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
    \item \textbf{Severability}: If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
    \item \textbf{Notices}: All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, certified mail, or email to the addresses specified above or as updated by written notice.
    \item \textbf{Counterparts}: This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
\end{enumerate}
