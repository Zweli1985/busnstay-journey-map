About Wazi Educational Support

Wazi Educational Support is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to fostering educational excellence and community empowerment in South Africa. Established with a vision to bridge educational gaps, we focus on capacity building, skills development, and inclusive education across rural and urban landscapes. Our mission is rooted in transparency, professionalism, and accountability, ensuring that every program we deliver creates lasting impact. With a team of highly qualified specialists, we design tailor-made training solutions that address the unique challenges faced by educators, students, and community leaders.

Our work spans a wide range of initiatives, from early childhood development to advanced teacher training, all aimed at building a stronger, more educated society. We collaborate with local stakeholders to ensure our programs are culturally relevant and sustainable, making education accessible to all, regardless of background or ability.

Our Comprehensive Services

Teacher Development Programs

Enhance teaching skills with our SACE-endorsed teacher training courses from Pre-Grade R to Grade 12, offering credits for professional development. Our programs focus on inclusive teaching strategies, curriculum innovation, and classroom management techniques, empowering teachers to create dynamic learning environments that cater to diverse student needs.

Early Childhood Development (ECD) Training

Support the foundation of learning with our ECD programs, designed for practitioners, caregivers, and educators. We offer short courses with certificates and SAQA and NQF Level 4 qualifications, focusing on child psychology, developmental milestones, and creative learning activities to ensure young learners receive the best start in life.

Youth Empowerment and Leadership

Equip young individuals with leadership, entrepreneurial, and life skills through interactive workshops and mentorship programs. Our initiatives foster community engagement and prepare youth for future challenges with confidence and competence.

Disability and Inclusion Training

Promote equity with our specialized training in sign language, accessibility, and inclusive education practices. We support differently-abled learners and educators, ensuring every individual has the opportunity to thrive in an inclusive setting.

Parent and Community Workshops

Engage families with educational workshops on child development, parenting skills, and community involvement. These sessions strengthen the support system around learners, fostering a collaborative approach to education.

Technology Integration in Education

Bridge the digital divide with our training on integrating technology into teaching and learning. We provide hands-on sessions on digital tools, e-learning platforms, and tech-supported curricula to modernize education delivery.
