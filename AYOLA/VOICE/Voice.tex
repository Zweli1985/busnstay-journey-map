\section*{Programme and Budget Overview}

\begin{infobox}[Purpose and Approach]
This programme, led by THE VOICE Research Department, documents Gukurahundi through survivor testimony, community dialogues and documentary evidence, while simultaneously collecting legally useful material for a dedicated legal team. It applies mixed research methods -- qualitative, quantitative and documentary review -- supported by secure IT systems so that evidence is traceable, verifiable and usable in legal processes.
\end{infobox}

\begin{infobox}[Budget Structure]
The budget covers the core requirements of THE VOICE Research Department and is organised into three pillars: (1) Capital equipment and assets for robust fieldwork and secure storage, (2) IT and communications for encrypted evidence handling and analysis, and (3) operational working capital for personnel, travel, accommodation and community processes. The graph below provides a high-level picture before the detailed line-item breakdowns.
\end{infobox}
