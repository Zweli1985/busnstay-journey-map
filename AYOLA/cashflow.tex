\section{Introduction}

\begin{tcolorbox}
This document provides detailed cash flow forecasts for Likhanyise Project (Pty) Ltd over a five-year period.
Year 1 is presented monthly, while Years 2 to 5 are presented on an annual basis.

All figures are expressed in South African Rand (R) and are prepared using conservative assumptions aligned
with the grant funding application.
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{0.3cm}

\begin{tcolorbox}[title=\textbf{Key Assumptions}]
\begin{itemize}
\item Cost of Sales equals 60\% of revenue:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Materials: 35\%
  \item Labour: 15\%
  \item Equipment Hire: 10\%
  \end{itemize}
\item Fixed operating expenses split as:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Administration: 60\%
  \item Other operating expenses: 40\%
  \end{itemize}
\item Grant funding of R250,000 received in February 2026.
\item Asset acquisitions:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Year 1: R191,725
  \item Year 3: R600,000
  \item Year 5: R1,200,000
  \end{itemize}
\item Opening cash balance is zero.
\item Cash flows prepared using the direct method.
\end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
