Our Shows

Khanya Community Radio offers a variety of shows designed to engage our listenership of over 150,000 across Butterworth, Kei Mouth, Kentani, Willowvale, Idutywa, Tsomo, Ngqamakhwe, parts of Elliotdale, and parts of Engcobo. Our programs promote community dialogue, educational content, and local talent, aligning with our mission to communicate values and support socio-economic development.
