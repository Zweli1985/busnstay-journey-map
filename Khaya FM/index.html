Late Night Tunes - Thobekile B. Khanyile

Thobekile is an ambitious Public Relations student from Mtubatuba, KwaZulu-Natal, currently pursuing her degree at Walter Sisulu University and gaining practical experience through Work-Integrated Learning at Khanya Community Radio.

View Bio

Thobekile B. Khanyile is a determined and ambitious individual hailing from the picturesque town of Mtubatuba in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. Born and raised in the vibrant community of Zwenelitsha, Thobekile's journey is a testament to her resilience and passion for personal growth. Currently pursuing a degree in Public Relations at Walter Sisulu University, she is honing her skills through Work-Integrated Learning at Khanya Community Radio, where she hosts the "Late Night Tunes" show, bringing soulful music and engaging content to listeners.
