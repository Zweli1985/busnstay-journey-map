About Pace Outdoor

Pace Outdoor is a premier South African outdoor advertising company that curates and manages high-impact advertising placements across building facades, billboards, wall surfaces, and distinctive architectural structures. Operating throughout South Africa’s major urban centres and key commercial corridors, we specialise in maximising brand visibility through strategically positioned outdoor media placements, serving as your dedicated partner in identifying, securing, and managing premium advertising locations that deliver exceptional audience engagement and brand exposure.

Our Strategic Approach

Our success is built on a meticulous site selection process that goes beyond traditional advertising placement. We conduct comprehensive analysis of each potential location, evaluating factors including traffic patterns, demographic alignment, visibility angles, and audience dwell time to ensure optimal return on your advertising investment.

Premium Location Portfolio

Pace Outdoor maintains an exclusive portfolio of carefully curated advertising spaces across diverse urban and suburban environments, designed to capture attention and drive engagement.

Building Facades

Client-Centric Partnership

At Pace Outdoor, we don’t simply rent advertising space – we deliver comprehensive outdoor advertising solutions tailored to each client’s specific objectives. Our team works collaboratively with your marketing team to align outdoor placements with broader campaign strategies, ensuring seamless integration across all marketing channels. Our end-to-end service includes location scouting, permit acquisition, installation coordination, campaign monitoring, and performance analytics, allowing you to focus on your core business whilst we maximise your outdoor advertising impact.
